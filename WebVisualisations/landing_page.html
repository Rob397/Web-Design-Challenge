Summary: Latitude Vs X

The purpose of theis project was to see how the weather changes as you get closer to the equator. We investigated the relationship between latitude and weather for the month of June. The team used humidity, temperauture, cloudiness and windspeed as variables to invetsigate the ipmact of the latitude on the weather conditions this time of year.
