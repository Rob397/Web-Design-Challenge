Summary

The purpose of this project was to see how the weather changes as you get closer to the equator. We investigated the relationship between latitude and weather for the month of June. The team used humidity, temperature, cloudiness and windspeed as variables to investigate the impact of the latitude on the weather conditions.



The results show that most world cities in this set have a humidity above and that higher latitude areas have more moderate temperatures. There are no visual patterns for levels of cloudiness and latitude. However, a different sample could show different results, especially when it is dependent on the weather patterns of the particular time of year.
